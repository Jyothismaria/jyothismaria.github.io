About me

I'm a data professional who genuinely loves turning messy, chaotic information into something clear, structured, and actually useful. Whether I'm profiling HPC jobs, building star-schema warehouses, or trying to convince Tableau not to break my filters again, I'm happiest when I'm solving problems with logic, creativity, and way too many color-coded notes.

I get excited about everything from clustering models to GANs to building dashboards that make people go, "Ohhh, that actually makes sense now." I enjoy digging into the why behind patterns, documenting every step (because future me deserves clarity), and designing solutions that feel clean, intuitive, and grounded in real data behavior.

At my core, I'm a curious, detail-obsessed data person who loves learning new tools, simplifying complexity, and using data to answer questions that matter — whether in HPC, analytics, or anywhere data decides to get interesting.
